About this webapp

This webapp is a way to play Alone Among The Stars in your browser.

Alone Among The Stars is a solo roleplaying game about exploring fantastic planets

You are a solitary adventurer, hopping from planet to planet exploring. Each world has unique features for you to discover and record.

Normally you'd flip cards and roll dice, but this webapp handles that for you.

For each planet you discover one or more things: you will get suggestions on what you discover and how, but ultimately you record what you find.

You can save a log of your travel and look at other people's logs, if they have shared them.
